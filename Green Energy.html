What is Green Energy?

Clean or green energies are those that do not produce any type of pollution or

CO2 emissions when generated. In addition, many of them, although not all, do not run out, that is, they are renewable.

Benefits about green energy

Clean energy

Inexhaustible energy sources

No polluting emissions or greenhouse gases

Energy independence

Self-consumption

Sustainability Environmental protection

Types of green energy

Solar energy

Wind energy

Hydraulic energy

Biomass energy

Tidal energy

Geothermal energy

Why to use green energy.

Because they are inexhaustible sources of energy, because you fight against climate change, because green energy does not emit polluting gases or radioactive waste.

By choosing green energy you reduce your environmental footprint and CO2 emissions into the atmosphere.
